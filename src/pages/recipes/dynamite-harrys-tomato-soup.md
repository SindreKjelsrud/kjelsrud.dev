**Prep Time:** 10 minutes

**Cook Time:** 30 minutes

**Servings:** 4

### Ingredients:

- 1 onion
- 1 carrot
- 1 celery stalk
- 2 cloves garlic
- 2 chilies
- 2 tbsp tomato paste
-  2 cans of diced tomatoes
-  0.5 l vegetable broth
-  1 tsp sugar or honey
-  2 tbsp oregano
-  Salt
- Pepper

### Cooking Directions:

- **Prepare vegetables:** Finely chop the onion, carrot, celery, garlic, and chilies.
- **Sauté vegetables:** In a pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped vegetables and sauté for 3 minutes.
- **Add tomato paste:** Stir in the tomato paste and let it cook for an additional minute.
- **Add liquids and herbs:** Pour in the vegetable broth and diced tomatoes, then add oregano. Stir to combine.
- **Cook:** Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce heat and let it simmer for about 20 minutes, or until all the vegetables are tender.
- **Blend:** Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth.
- **Season:** Add sugar or honey, salt, and pepper to taste.
- **Serve:** Top with fresh basil and cooked macaroni if desired.
