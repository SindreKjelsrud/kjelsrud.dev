**Prep Time:** 10 minutes

**Cook Time:** 20 minutes

**Servings:** 4

### Ingredients:

- 500g chicken
- Rice
- Rapeseed oil or sunflower oil
- 1 red bell pepper
- 1 green bell pepper
- 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
- 1 tsp ginger, finely chopped
- Sauce
	- 2-3 tbsp kecap manis
	- 2-3 tbsp sweet chili sauce
	- 1 tsp sesame oil

### Cooking Directions:

- **Prepare vegetables:** Slice the bell peppers into long strips and finely chop the garlic and ginger.
- **Prepare chicken:** Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces.
- **Make sauce:** Mix the sauce ingredients (kecap manis, sweet chili sauce, and sesame oil) in a small bowl.
- **Cook chicken:** In a pan, heat the oil and sauté the chicken. After a few minutes, add the garlic and ginger.
- **Add vegetables:** Once the chicken is golden brown, add the bell peppers.
- **Add sauce:** Pour the sauce over the chicken and vegetables, stirring well to coat the chicken thoroughly.
- **Serve:** Serve the sweet chicken over a bed of rice.
