**Prep Time:** 5 minutes

**Cook Time:** 10-15 minutes 

**Servings:** 2-3

### Ingredients:

- 3 dl water
- 2-3 dl chosen berry 
- 2-3 tbsp potato starch + water

### Cooking Directions:

- Boil chosen berry and water 
- Remove jam from the heat
- Pour in the potato starch mixture
- Return the mixture to the heat and stir 
- As soon as it boils, remove the pot from the heat
