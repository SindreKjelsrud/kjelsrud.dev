**Prep Time:** 15 minutes

**Cook Time:** 30 minutes

**Servings:** 4

### Ingredients:

- 450g chicken
- 2 carrots
- 1 apple
- 2 onions
- 2 chilies (adjust according to heat preference)
- 2 tbsp garam masala
- 2 cans diced tomatoes
- 500 ml water or chicken broth
- 100 ml cooking cream
- 2 vegetable bouillon cubes
- Salt, pepper, and a pinch of sugar

### Cooking Directions:

- **Prepare vegetables:** Peel and dice the carrots, apple, and onions. Finely chop the chilies.
- **Sauté vegetables:** In a pot, sauté the carrots, apple, onions, chilies, and garam masala over medium heat until the vegetables are translucent and tender.
- **Add liquids:** Add the diced tomatoes, water or chicken broth, and bouillon cubes to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
- **Finish the soup:** Stir in the cooking cream and season with additional garam masala, sugar, salt, and pepper to taste.
- **Cook chicken:** Dice the chicken, season with salt and pepper, and sauté in a separate pan until fully cooked.
- **Combine:** Add the cooked chicken pieces to the soup just before serving.
