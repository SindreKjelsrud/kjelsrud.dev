> 🔗 **https://trinesmatblogg.no/2010/03/vafler/**

**Prep Time:** 10 minutes

**Cook Time:** 15 minutes 

**Servings:** 4-6

### Ingredients:

- 2 eggs (use egg substitute if desired)
- 2 dl sour cream
- 3 dl milk
- 1 dl sugar
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1 tsp vanilla sugar
- 1 tsp cardamom
- 100 g butter, melted
- approximately 350 g flour

### Cooking Directions:

- In a bowl, mix eggs, sour cream, milk, and sugar together.
- Then, stir in the dry ingredients until you have a lump-free batter.
- Stir in the melted butter and let the batter rest for at least 15 minutes.
- Cook the mixture in a waffle iron.
