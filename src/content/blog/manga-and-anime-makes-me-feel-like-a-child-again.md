growing up I watched and read lot of cartoons and comics. stuff like [samurai jack](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Samurai_Jack) and [donald pockets](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Donald_Duck_pocket_books) were my go-to entertainment, besides [pokemon fire red](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pok%C3%A9mon_FireRed_and_LeafGreen) on my gameboy micro, every day. they sparked a lot of different emotions inside me!

but, unfortunately, I grew apart from it as my peers stopped talking about it, calling it lame and so on.

lucky me, around 6 years ago, I was introduced to anime and manga and I've been hooked ever since. it gives me the same feelings I got from cartoons and comics when I was younger, especially [shonen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sh%C5%8Dnen_manga) and [seinen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seinen_manga).

I just love it. there's so much to choose from now, and I can get lost reading volume upon volume.
